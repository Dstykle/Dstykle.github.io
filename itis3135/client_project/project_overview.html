Website Overview

Purpose

My stepdad has been flooring ceramic tiles and wooden floors for years as a professional. He has done many projects and always followed through with it and I have helped and seen what he has done. He has completed jobs in people's living rooms, businesses, and even college campuses. His only way of finding people is through word of mouth but with this website, he will be able to share and promote his business to other clients more easily.

Who will be using this site

Clients that he works with and new ones that check out his work and how to reach him.

Categories

  • Home Page(index.html): This is an introduction page to show who Felipe is and how long he has been in the industry. Here we will show services provided and Other information like how to contact him and where he is available will also be here. All clients can view this.
  • Ceramic or Wooden Flooring(flooring.html): This page will be about the differences between ceramic and wooden flooring like how it looks when done/why people are interested in each. All clients can view this.
  • Client Project Showcase(Ceramic Tiling)(ceramic.html): Showcase of his most proudest work using ceramic tiles that Felipe will want to show his clients if this is what they are interested in. This can include photos. All clients can view this.
  • Client Project Showcase(Wooden Flooring)(wooden.html): Showcase of his most proudest work using wooden floors that Felipe will want to show his clients if this is what they are interested in. This can include photos. All clients can view this.
  • Contact Me(contact.html): This site will be a way for clients to email Felipe directly from here to let him know there is someone who is interested. All clients can view this.
