A Dive into Volleyball

Home

Volleyball is a sport where there are two teams of six people. There are many aspects to the game ranging from playing casually with some friends to playing competitively against a rival team. The main goal of the game is to keep the ball from touching the ground on your side of the net and scoring by hitting the ground of the opposing team. This sport is often played either indoors, outdoors in the sand, or even in the water as a friendly game.

Volleyball is a widely loved sport both in a competitive scene and casually. On this topic I will be focusing more on the competitive side of volleyball. Playing volleyball can take a lot out of you especially because it required you to be quick thinking, a team player, have well communication with your teammates, as well as being aware of your surroundings. Teams tend to analyze their opponents before matches in order to prepare for the best strategy to take them down.
