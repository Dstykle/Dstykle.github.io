A Dive into Volleyball

Home

Volleyball is a sport where there are two teams of six people. There are many aspects to the game ranging from playing casually with some friends to playing competitively against a rival team. The main goal of the game is to keep the ball from touching the ground on your side of the net and scoring by hitting the ground of the opposing team. This sport is often played either indoors, outdoors in the sand, or even in the water as a friendly game.

Volleyball is a widely loved sport both in a competitive scene and casually. On this topic I will be focusing more on the competitive side of volleyball. Playing volleyball can take a lot out of you especially because it required you to be quick thinking, a team player, have well communication with your teammates, as well as being aware of your surroundings. Teams tend to analyze their opponents before matches in order to prepare for the best strategy to take them down.

High School Volleyball Season Scheduling

High School Vollyball Season Description
Fall Season Volleyball is usually played in the fall season around the months of July or August. After practices during these months then follow competitive matches. Of course, this can vary by school.
Pre-Season Before the start of the season there are typically tryouts and practice sessions to determine who will be making the roster. Preparingby going to practice can helpthe player physically and mentally so they can know what to expect when they play and what they should look out for.
Regular Season During the regular season matches are played on a weekly basis. You can imagine how busy it can get when it consists of practices, matches, and tournaments. Which can run from around August to sometime around the end of November.
Postseason The postseason is when things start to get more wrapped up and the only games that are being played are tournaments.

College Volleyball Seasons Scheduling

College Vollyball Season Description
Divisions There are different divisions in volleyball based on competitive level between each one. These divisions are: Division I, Division II, and Division III. The most competitive division being Division I and the least being Division III.
NCAA Guidelines NCAA (National Collegiate Athletic Association) is the governing body for volleyball in the United States. They provide the regulations that schools have to follow in order to play in NCAA competitions during the volleyball season. This can include rules on the size of the court, the number of players, and the number of players per team. These rules can also be different for different divisions.
Regular Season The regular season is the main part of volleyball during the volleyball season. This can last from around the beginning of September to the end of the May. Since division I is the most competitive division, it is the longest season because they end up having to play much earlier than the other divisions. This makes their schedule much more intense as well since they expect them to be the top players.
Conference Play Conference play are matches that are played against teams grouped in the same league. They are essentially matches in a regulare season but they are played against teams in their respective conference. They do this so that they can put together teams of similar level to play against each other so no teams are being blown out of the water by another.
Postseason The postseason is usually between around late November to early December. During this time it mostly includes tournament matches and national championships.

Where to Look

three of the best locations....

Los Angeles, California



Here in Los Angeles is known for having a huge community that are into volleyball. They host an annual event called the Junior Olympics Volleyball tournament. They have great world class facilities that are well-maintained for training. This makes it a perfect area to stay for professional players looking to play in the top division.
Chicago, Illinois



The Chicago area have a strong volleyball culture within its community and is home to many professional players. Their gyms equip some of the best technology within its facilities. They also host many national level tournaments through the year.
Minneapolis, Minnesota



In the Minneapolis area, they take their volleyball game very seriously. They have big programs for the most top-tier players. When it comes to their facilities they are some of the best in the country. This city also hosts championships which attracts the players from all over the country to play.
